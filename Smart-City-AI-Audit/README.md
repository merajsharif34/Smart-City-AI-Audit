# Auditing Representation and Equity in Smart City AI Datasets

## Description

This project audits Smart City AI datasets to evaluate:

- Demographic representation
- Transportation behaviour
- Urban accessibility
- Socioeconomic equity

## Research Questions

RQ1: Who is represented in Smart City AI datasets?

RQ2: Whose transportation behaviours are captured?

RQ3: Are demographic groups equally served by urban infrastructure?

RQ4: Does socioeconomic status influence access to urban infrastructure?

RQ5: Which citizen behaviours dominate Smart City datasets?

## Datasets

1. smart_city_citizen_activity.csv

2. urban_connectivity.csv
